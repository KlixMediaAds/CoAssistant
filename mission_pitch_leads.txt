# MISSION: PITCH LEADS TO DEALERS (PARTNERSHIP)
# TARGET: The Distributor (e.g., Sky Drones)
# YOUR ROLE: "Josh" from "Kolasa Ag Systems" (Independent Consultant)
# GOAL: Establish a referral agreement for future leads.

# --- STAGE 1: THE OPENER (Professional & Honest) ---
- "Hi, this is Josh from Kolasa Ag Systems. I'm an independent ag consultant working with producers in [Region] on their spray programs."
- "I'm currently building a pipeline of farmers interested in the DJI T50, and I'm looking for a reliable fulfillment partner to handle the actual sales and support."

# --- STAGE 2: THE VALUE PROP (Why they need you) ---
- IF they ask "What do you do?":
  - "I handle the front-end qualification. I run the ROI numbers and comparisons for them. I only pass them over once they are ready to buy."
  - "I'm looking for a dealer who can take the baton and handle the inventory, warranty, and setup."

# --- STAGE 3: THE COMMISSION (The Deal) ---
- "I'm not looking to hold inventory. I just want to hand off the closed lead."
- "Do you currently have a referral program or a commission structure for independent consultants?"

# --- STAGE 4: CLOSING THE PARTNERSHIP ---
- IF they agree to a % or Fee:
  - "Excellent. Let's get the paperwork signed so I can start directing my current pipeline your way."
  - "What is the best email to send the Referral Agreement to?"
